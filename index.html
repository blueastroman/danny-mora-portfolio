How I think

Four principles I work by.

Strategy before story.

You can't find the right story until you know what the brand is trying to do. I start there.

Specificity is the strategy.

Broad briefs produce generic stories. The best ones I've built started with one true thing.

Earn attention, don't demand it.

A story no one asked for has to be worth the interruption. I hold everything to that standard.

The story says how it wants to be told.

Format, medium, creator, campaign. Those are answers to a question the story asks first.
