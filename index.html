How I think

A few things I believe about the work.

I like results. I also think the work usually gets better when the idea is clearer, the brief is tighter, and the people involved actually make sense for the brand.

Story before format

Platform trends come and go. A good idea lasts longer.

Relationships over transactions

Creator work gets better when it feels like a real partnership, not a one-off buy.

Premium needs precision

If a brand wants to feel premium, the details cannot be sloppy.

Complicated ideas need clear language

The more complicated the category, the clearer and more grounded the message should be.

Now

What I’m focused on right now.

Right now I’m especially interested in creator relationships, brand taste, and how technology is changing the way people decide what feels useful, credible, or worth their time.

I keep thinking about better briefs, better partnership fits, and the difference between marketing that gets seen and marketing that actually sticks.

I’m also paying attention to AI, mostly because it’s already changing how brands talk, how people search, and how quickly mediocre content is becoming invisible.
