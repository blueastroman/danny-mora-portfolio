Work | Daniel Mora

META DESCRIPTION
Selected work by Daniel Mora across creator strategy, brand marketing, and narrative production.

PAGE HERO
Eyebrow: Selected work
Headline: Brand work, creator partnerships, and a filmmaker's eye.
Intro: I've worked across global airline marketing, branded storytelling, and original film projects. The throughline is always the same: a clear idea, the right person to carry it, and work that actually lands.
Side note: I'm most useful in the middle of strategy and execution: creator selection, campaign structure, creative development, production, and the performance story after launch.

CASE STUDY 1
Kicker: Global creator marketing
Title: Lufthansa Group
Summary: Across Lufthansa, SWISS, and Austrian Airlines, I worked on creator and partnership campaigns built to make premium travel feel current, social, and worth talking about.
Paragraph 1: The work ranged from Pride and Fashion Week to destination storytelling, product launches, and year-round brand visibility. It required strong creator fit, sharp briefs, platform instincts, and constant judgment about what still felt right for the brand.
Paragraph 2: This is the kind of work I like most because it combines systems and taste. The process matters, the relationships matter, and the creative still has to hold up.
Bullets:
Topline: A 35-creator ecosystem producing 300+ assets, 8M+ organic views, and 30M+ paid impressions.
Program shape: Creator partnerships spanning product launches, destination storytelling, and cultural moments across multiple brands.
Platforms: Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube.
High-performing moments: Pride, Fashion Week, destination storytelling, ski races, jazz festival, and premium cabin launches.
Meta card 1: Scope / Creator strategy, Brand partnerships, Campaign planning, Performance storytelling
Meta card 2: Signals / 35 creators, 300+ assets, 8M+ organic views, 30M+ paid impressions, 3 platforms activated

CASE STUDY 2
Kicker: Purpose-led branded storytelling
Title: Brand New World Studios
Summary: Before creator strategy became a bigger part of my work, I spent years learning how to tell brand stories without sanding off the humanity.
Paragraph 1: At Brand New World Studios in Atlanta, I produced films about Oprah Winfrey, Little Miss Flint, Hershey, and the Salvation Army. Polished visuals weren't enough. The work needed a point of view.
Paragraph 2: That experience still shapes how I think as a marketer. A campaign can be organized, strategic, and well-targeted, but if it feels lifeless, people know.
Bullets:
Projects: Purpose-led branded films and documentaries across social, editorial, and longer-form formats.
Contribution: Creative development, production, narrative framing, and message discipline.
Takeaway: Story craft is a brand skill, not just a film skill.
Meta card 1: Selected stories / Oprah Winfrey purpose film, Little Miss Flint purpose film, Hershey purpose film, Salvation Army purpose film
Meta card 2: What it sharpened / Editorial judgment, Message clarity, Production fluency, Emotional storytelling

CASE STUDY 3
Kicker: Original work and post-production
Title: Independent film, documentary, and VFX
Summary: Most of my creative instincts were built here. The marketing brain came later.
Paragraph 1: I've written, directed, and produced original short films including The Happiness Machine and A Trip to Remember, co-wrote and co-directed the documentary A New Coke, and worked in VFX supervision and editing on Baba I'm Fine, an SXSW Official Selection in 2025.
Paragraph 2: Those projects taught me how to solve creative problems quickly, keep people aligned around a story, and work through constraints without panicking.
Bullets:
Original films: Writing, directing, producing, editing, and remote collaboration.
VFX: Supervision, editorial precision, and post-production problem solving.
Awards: Kiki McCabe and Agnes Nixon Screenwriting Award at Emory University.
Meta card 1: Selected credits / The Happiness Machine, A Trip to Remember, A New Coke, Baba I'm Fine
Meta card 2: What it taught me / Creative leadership, Resourcefulness, Taste under pressure, Audience empathy
