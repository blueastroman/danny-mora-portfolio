CASE STUDY 1
Title: Dirk Nowitzki Partnership
Label: Ambassador
A 7-foot MVP is not a brand fit for every airline. For Lufthansa Allegris, he was the only choice.
We built a global ambassador partnership around a single idea: scale deserves scale. The campaign ran across OOH and CTV, anchored by creative that matched the product's ambition.

CASE STUDY 2
Title: Creator Program
Label: Influencer
Most influencer programs are built around reach. This one was built around fit.
We identified exactly the kind of creator Lufthansa's audience trusts, found the best ones, and gave them full creative control. Creators know their audience better than any brief does and the results proved it.
2025 Results: 35 creator trips. 300+ assets. 8M+ organic views at a 24.7% view rate against a 17% benchmark. 5.27% engagement rate against a 4.01% benchmark.

CASE STUDY 3
Title: New England Patriots
Label: Partnership
A three-year partnership built around a simple premise: bring American football to Berlin and bring Boston fans with it.
We activated in Berlin, ran a sweepstakes that flew winners from Boston to an NFL game in Germany, and built a program that grows every year. Lufthansa as the bridge between two cultures that are still figuring each other out.

CASE STUDY 5
Title: Film Background
Label: Before Lufthansa

Before aviation marketing, I was in production. VFX editor and supervisor on films that went to festivals. Baba I'm Fine, a short directed by Karina Dandashi, selected for SXSW 2025. Jungle Rhythms, directed by Adrian Kader, making the festival circuit. A TV pilot with director Rob Schmidt.

At Brand New World Studios in Atlanta, that same instinct moved into branded work. Purpose-led documentaries about Oprah Winfrey, Little Miss Flint, Hershey's, and the Salvation Army. Films that started with why.

I also won the Kiki McCabe and Agnes Nixon Screenwriting Award at Emory University for my screenplay Tightrope, presented by Pulitzer Prize-winning poet Dr. Jericho Brown.
