Selected work

Brand systems, creator partnerships, and story craft.

I’ve worked across global airline marketing, purpose-driven branded storytelling, and original film projects. The throughline is usually the same: figure out what the story is, who it matters to, and how to make it land with clarity.

I’m most useful where strategy and execution meet: creator selection, brand partnerships, creative development, production, campaign structure, and the performance story that comes after the launch.

Global creator marketing

Lufthansa Group

Across Lufthansa, SWISS, and Austrian Airlines, I worked on brand and partnership efforts that made premium travel feel contemporary, social, and culture-connected.

The work balanced major tentpole moments like Pride and Fashion Week with destination storytelling, premium product launches, and steady brand visibility throughout the year. It required a mix of creator fit, platform instincts, strong briefing, and careful attention to what still felt true to the brand.

What I like most about this kind of work is the systems thinking behind it: the creator ecosystem, the messaging discipline, the relationship management, and the structure that makes a premium brand feel native on social without diluting its tone.

  • 2025 organic snapshot: 31 creators, 297 social posts, 5.7M organic views, 316K engagements.
  • 2025 total delivery: 24.7M paid and organic views or impressions, plus 330K total engagements.
  • Benchmarks: 24.7% organic view rate versus 17% median benchmark and 5.27% engagement rate versus 4.01% benchmark.
  • High-performing moments: Pride, Fashion Week, destination storytelling, ski races, jazz festival, and premium cabin launches.

Purpose-led branded storytelling

Brand New World Studios

Before creator strategy became a big part of my work, I spent years learning how to tell brand stories with emotional precision.

At Brand New World Studios in Atlanta, I produced purpose-driven films about Oprah Winfrey, Little Miss Flint, Hershey, and the Salvation Army. These projects asked for more than polished visuals. They needed a clear point of view, a strong narrative spine, and the discipline to make brand values feel earned instead of scripted.

That experience still shapes how I think as a marketer. Good campaigns are not just technically correct. They have rhythm, humanity, and a reason for existing in the first place.

  • Projects: Purpose-led branded films and documentaries across social, editorial, and longer-form formats.
  • Contribution: Creative development, production, narrative framing, and message discipline.
  • Takeaway: Story craft is a brand skill, not just a film skill.

Original work & post-production

Independent film, documentary, and VFX

My film background is not separate from my marketing work. It is where my taste, pacing, and instinct for detail came from.

I’ve written, directed, and produced original short films including The Happiness Machine and A Trip to Remember, co-wrote and co-directed the documentary A New Coke, and worked in VFX supervision and editing on projects including Baba I’m Fine, an SXSW Official Selection in 2025.

Those projects taught me how to solve creative problems under pressure, how to keep a team aligned around a story, and how to make constraints produce better work instead of weaker work.

  • Original films: Writing, directing, producing, editing, and remote collaboration.
  • VFX: Supervision, editorial precision, and post-production problem solving.
  • Awards: Kiki McCabe and Agnes Nixon Screenwriting Award at Emory University.
