About

Marketing manager leading integrated campaigns and partnerships for global brands.

I’m a Marketing Manager at Lufthansa in New York, where I lead influencer and partnership marketing across entertainment, sports, and travel.

Since 2022, I’ve built a global creator strategy delivering consistent 10x+ ROI and executed campaigns and partnerships with brands and partners including The Walt Disney Company, the New England Patriots, Dirk Nowitzki, and American Express.

Based in New York and originally from Costa Rica, I like work that blends creative storytelling, brand strategy, and marketing tactics without losing the human part.

My story

The current chapter is the clearest one.

My work now sits at the intersection of integrated marketing, creator partnerships, and brand storytelling. At Lufthansa, that means building campaigns across entertainment, sports, and travel, then making sure the partnerships, content, and performance all work together as one system.

Alongside that, I still work in visual effects on short films recognized at major festivals, including SXSW and NFFTY. I’ve served as a Visual Effects Supervisor on projects for multiple filmmakers, and that craft background still shapes how I think about pacing, clarity, and story.

The throughline is pretty simple: I like building work that can perform at scale without feeling generic.
