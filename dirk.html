A Seat Fit for an MVP

A global ambassador partnership for Lufthansa Allegris answering one simple question: how can a 7ft MVP basketball player sleep comfortably on a plane?

Starting in 2024, we partnered with NBA legend Dirk Nowitzki as Lufthansa's official brand ambassador to promote the new Lufthansa Allegris. One of the additions was a seat with an extra-long bed that measures 7 ft 2 in, enough for a 7-foot MVP with room to spare.

What began as a cold call from Dirk’s team turned into an ongoing partnership with one of the most iconic German athletes of all time. Dirk’s creative has since been featured in campaigns across the world, from San Francisco and Chicago to Munich and Tokyo.

Why it worked

A simple idea with real fit.

The strength of the work was how clean the idea was. Lufthansa had a premium product story to tell, and Dirk made the extra-long bed instantly legible in a way that felt memorable instead of over-explained.

It also had staying power. This was not a one-off appearance. It became a longer ambassador relationship that could travel across markets while still feeling tied to a real product truth.
